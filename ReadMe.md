## 👋 About Me

I'm a final-year **Software Engineering** student at **Mehran University of Engineering and Technology**, graduating **December 2026**, and a backend-leaning full-stack developer currently building production systems at **ZETSOL Technologies**.

I care about the parts of engineering that don't show up in a demo — clean API contracts, auth that doesn't leak edge cases, and queries that stay fast once real data shows up. My background spans **React / Next.js** on the frontend and **Node.js, NestJS, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB** on the backend, with hands-on experience shipping REST APIs, JWT-secured systems, RBAC, and search-heavy e-commerce features to production.

I'm most interested in the space between *"it works"* and *"it's engineered right"* — and I enjoy being the person who closes that gap.
